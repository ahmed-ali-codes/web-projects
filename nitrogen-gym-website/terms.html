Operating Rules

Terms of Service.

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

Welcome to Nitrogen. By enrolling in a membership or entering our facilities, you agree to the following terms and conditions. These rules are designed to ensure the safety, performance, and elite culture of our community.

1. Physical Readiness

Members must complete a physical readiness questionnaire (PAR-Q) and an initial performance assessment before using Nitrogen-specific recovery technology (Advanced recovery tools) or participating in Apex training sessions. You agree that you are in good physical health or have been cleared by a physician for high-intensity training.

2. Facility Conduct

Nitrogen is a performance environment. Respect for equipment and other athletes is mandatory. This includes re-racking all weights, cleaning equipment after use, and adhering to our "zero intimidation" coaching guidelines. Failure to follow staff directions regarding safety may result in immediate revocation of access.

3. Membership & Cancellation

Memberships are billed monthly on your initiation date. You may freeze your membership for up to 30 days once per year. Cancellation requires a 14-day notice via the member portal. Note that some promotional tiers may have locked contract periods as specified at the time of purchase.

4. Liability Release

You acknowledge that high-intensity training and the use of specialized recovery equipment involve inherent risks. By using the facility, you release Nitrogen Gym and its staff from liability for injuries sustained on the premises, provided they are not caused by gross negligence of the facility.

5. Modifications

Nitrogen reserves the right to modify class schedules, staff rosters, and equipment availability to ensure the highest quality of service. We will provide 48-hour notice for any major changes to class timetables.
