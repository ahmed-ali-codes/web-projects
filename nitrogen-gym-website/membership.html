Questions

Common Queries.

Is 1-on-1 personal training available?
Absolutely. We offer premium 1-on-1 coaching with our elite trainers. You can book individual sessions or opt for our Elite or Apex membership tiers which include PT sessions as part of the package.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your tier at any time through our member portal. Changes will take effect on your next billing cycle.
Do you offer guest passes?
Yes! Every Nitrogen member gets 2 guest passes per month to bring a training partner along. Additional passes can be purchased at the front desk.
Are there any hidden fees?
No hidden fees. We believe in transparency. Your monthly rate includes everything listed in your tier. No sign-up or cancellation fees.
