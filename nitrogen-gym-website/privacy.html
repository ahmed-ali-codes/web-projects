Privacy Protocol

Data Protection.

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

At Nitrogen Gym, we prioritize the protection of your performance data and personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data within our physical facilities and digital training platforms.

1. Information We Collect

To provide elite-level training, we collect several types of information:

  • Biometric Data: Body composition, heart rate, and performance metrics collected via our integrated training systems.
  • Health Information: Medical history and physical limitations shared during your initial consultation to ensure safe training protocols.
  • Personal Details: Name, contact information, and billing details required for membership management.
  • Usage Data: Timing and frequency of your facility access and session bookings.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used strictly to enhance your training experience:

  • Optimizing your personalized training and nutrition protocols.
  • Tracking performance progress over time in local dashboards.
  • Managing membership cycles and facility access.
  • Communicating critical updates regarding gym equipment or class schedules.

3. Data Security

Nitrogen utilizes military-grade encryption for all performance data storage. Your biometric information is never sold to third-party insurance or marketing companies. Access to your performance metrics is restricted to your assigned coaching staff and Dr. Kai Reeves' recovery team.

4. Your Rights

Members have the right to request a full export of their performance data at any time. You may also request the deletion of your training history upon membership termination, though some billing records must be retained for legal compliance.

5. Contact

For questions regarding our privacy protocol, contact privacy@nitrogen-gym.com.
