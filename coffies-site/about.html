Who We Are

The Coffies Story

A passion project born from one obsession: the perfect cup of coffee.

How It Started

From a Single Cup to a Global Mission

In 2018, our founder Maria Chen returned from a trip to Ethiopia with a small bag of hand-picked Yirgacheffe beans and an idea: what if specialty coffee didn't have to be complicated, expensive, or exclusive?

She started Coffies from her kitchen table — roasting small batches, learning every variable, and sharing cups with neighbours. Word spread. Orders came. The kitchen became a roastery. The roastery became a movement.

Today, Coffies works directly with over 30 farms across 12 countries, employing fair-trade practices and investing in the communities that grow our beans.
